YouTube Music

Experience YouTube Music on your classic iOS devices with style.

✨ Description

Stream your favorite music, playlists, and enjoy background playback on older iPhones and iPads. Designed specifically for iOS 6, lightweight and optimized, with a visually consistent YouTube Red aesthetic that feels right at home.

🎵 Features

  • Stream and discover millions of songs with seamless playback
  • Background playback while using other apps or when screen is locked
  • Lightweight and optimized specifically for legacy iOS devices
  • Simple and clean UI with authentic YouTube Red styling
  • Full compatibility with iOS 6 (iPhone 4S, iPad 2/3/4)
  • Completely free with no subscriptions or premium requirements
  • Offline playlist support for your downloaded music
  • Battery-optimized playback for extended listening sessions
