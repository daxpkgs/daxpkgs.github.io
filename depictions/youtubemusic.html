YouTube Music for Legacy iOS

Experience YouTube Music on your classic iOS devices.

Description

Stream your favorite music, playlists, and enjoy background playback on older iPhones and iPads. Designed for iOS 6, lightweight, and visually consistent with the original YouTube Red aesthetic.

Features

  • Stream and discover millions of songs with ease
  • Background playback while using other apps
  • Lightweight, optimized for legacy iOS devices
  • Simple and clean UI with YouTube Red styling
  • Works on iOS 6 (iPhone 4S, iPad 2/3/4)
  • Completely free, no subscriptions required
