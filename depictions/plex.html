Plex

Access your Plex server's media effortlessly on older iOS devices.

✨ Description

Stream or download movies, TV shows, and trailers with smooth browsing of popular and trending content. Designed specifically for iOS 6 — combining modern usability with classic simplicity and optimized performance for vintage hardware.

🎯 Features

  • Stream or download your Plex server's local media for offline enjoyment
  • Browse popular movies and TV shows with rich artwork and metadata
  • Stay up to date with trending movie trailers and new releases
  • Resume playback seamlessly across all your devices
  • Offline mode with pre-synced content for anywhere access
  • Fast, lightweight app built specifically for legacy iOS performance
  • Completely free — no subscriptions, ads, or hidden fees
  • Optimized for iPhone 4S, iPad 2/3/4, and newer legacy devices
