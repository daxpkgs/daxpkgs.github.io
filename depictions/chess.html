Chess

Master the board with this classic Chess app for legacy iOS devices.

Description

Engage in the timeless game of Chess on your older iOS devices. Play against a challenging AI, hone your skills, and enjoy a clean, intuitive interface optimized for classic hardware.

Features

  • Classic Chess gameplay with standard rules
  • Multiple AI difficulty levels to challenge all players
  • Intuitive and clean user interface
  • Smooth performance optimized for legacy iOS devices (iOS 6+)
  • No internet connection required for single-player
  • Completely free and open-source
